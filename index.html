As healthy as possible.

A non-profit focused on POLR2A

Research

It is our goal to make sure research that benefits those with a POLR2A gene mutation is continually visible and a driving force to academic institutions.

Education

We endeavor to share and make the most information available to doctors, caregivers, and those interested in learning more about the POLR2A gene.

You

We are ambitious about being able to reach out to you, the people affected by mutations of the POLR2A gene, and be able to help you be As Healthy As Possible.
